Privacy Policy for Pub Games

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the mobile application Pub Games on iOS devices. The Application is a local multiplayer and "pass-the-phone" party game designed for groups.

1. Data Controller

As the Application operates entirely offline on your device, we do not collect or store your personal data. For any local data stored on your device, the Data Controller is the end-user. If you have any inquiries, contact the developer at idan.avramov96@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect (or Do Not Collect)

  • No Central Server Collection: We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data (such as names, emails, IP addresses, or device IDs) on external servers.
  • Offline / Local Storage: All game configurations, player names, and custom packs are saved locally on your device using Apple's secure internal storage (UserDefaults).
  • Local Network Usage: Uses Multipeer Connectivity locally to discover and connect nearby devices. This connection is peer-to-peer and does not transmit data over the internet.
  • Photo Library Access & Peer-to-Peer Sharing: For the "Photo Roulette" game, the Application requests permission to access your local Photo Library. Randomly selected photos are transmitted directly and exclusively to the other players' devices in your local game session over a secure local network connection (Wi-Fi/Bluetooth) so they can appear on their screens for the guessing game.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

Your local device processes data based on the consent you grant via iOS permission dialogs (e.g., local network or photo access). You can revoke consent in Settings at any time.

4. Data Sharing and Transfer

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or transfer any data or photos to third parties, advertising networks, or analytics services.
  • Temporary Peer-to-Peer Transfer: Photos and player names are transferred strictly between the participant devices in the local game room. Photos are cached temporarily in memory during the round to display the gameplay and are discarded when the game ends. They are never uploaded to any remote server or cloud database.

5. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Because all data is stored locally on your own device, you have full control over it. You can erase all player data and custom packs at any time by simply uninstalling the Application from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The Application does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact
